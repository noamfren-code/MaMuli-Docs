# Privacy Policy for MaMuli **Last updated:** January 3, 2026 ## 1. Introduction This Privacy Policy explains how MaMuli ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information about you. This policy applies to your access and use of our mobile application (the "App"). ## 2. Information We Collect ### 2.1 Location Data We collect precise location data (GPS) to enable the core functionality of the App: Augmented Reality navigation. * **Purpose**: To calculate the distance and bearing to nearby settlements. * **Storage**: This data is processed locally on your device in real-time. We do not store your location history on our servers. * **Sharing**: Precise location data is not shared with third parties, except as required for the delivery of advertisements via Google AdMob (see Section 3). ### 2.2 Camera Usage We use your device's camera to display the Augmented Reality (AR) overlay. * **Processing**: Camera images are processed locally on your device (glued to the screen view). * **Recording**: We do not record or upload video/images to cloud servers. Use of the "Snapshot/Share" feature captures a static image to your device's local storage for you to share via other apps at your discretion. ## 3. Third-Party Services & Ads ### 3.1 Google AdMob We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use: * Device Identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads. * Coarse Location data to serve relevant ads. * Cookies or similar technologies. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and California, we provide a Consent Management Platform (UMP) to allow you to manage your preferences regarding personalized ads. * [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads) ### 3.2 Overpass API We query the OpenStreetMap (OSM) database via the Overpass API to fetch settlement data. * **Data Sent**: Your current latitude/longitude coordinates and search radius. * **Purpose**: To retrieve the names and locations of nearby towns. * **Privacy**: These queries are anonymous and strictly for data retrieval. ## 4. Children’s Privacy Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. ## 5.
